Our Vision and Mission

Vision

"Velocita Racing envisions to create a sustainable environment in motorsports by developing competitive emission-free vehicles."

Mission

"Velocita Racing aims to build well-engineered and reliable electric vehicles by the core involvement of students, professors, and industrial experts through cutting-edge technologies and innovations."

About Us

Team Velocita Racing is a crew of young engineers striving for excellence in automotive innovation. Each member contributes unique ideas and designs, bringing them to life through cutting-edge engineering practices and state-of-the-art facilities.

Since 2016, we've been pushing the boundaries in electric vehicle technology, transforming Velocita from a project into a proud tradition at PSG.

PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research

PSG iTech, the latest initiative of PSG and Sons' Charities, is dedicated to empowering youth through technical education. We offer various engineering disciplines, focusing on innovative learning, industry engagement, and ethical practices.

In a short time, PSG iTech has become one of the most sought-after institutions for engineering aspirants in Tamil Nadu.

About the Competition

Formula Bharat

Formula Bharat is India's premier Formula Student-style competition, promoting practical engineering experience and student innovation. It offers a competitive platform for both Indian and international teams, providing unparalleled exposure and learning opportunities in automotive engineering.

Achievements

Outcomes of the Competition
